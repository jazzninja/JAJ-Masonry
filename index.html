ABOUT

A family business founded in 1988 which has proven itself repeatedly as one of the best specialty contractors in the industry. With unmatched industry knowledge, our team is a leader in stone consulting, sourcing and installation. Our specialties include natural stone and tile installation, masonry, restoration, and waterproofing.

Our global resources for stone sourcing are widespread. Including exclusive partnering with The Santucci Group in Carrara Italy for the Boston Market. From value engineering, through material selection and installation, our team is experienced in the complete project process leaving no stone unturned.

As a certified Women Owned Business Enterprise (WBE) and member of the Marble Institute of America we have worked with many of the giants of general contractors in New England. As a Signatory Union Contractor we uphold the highest standards on every project and our dedication shows in every stone we lay.
